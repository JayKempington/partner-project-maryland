The mission of the University of Maryland is to provide excellent teaching, research and service. The university educates students and advances knowledge in areas of importance to the state, nation and world.

Student Life

More than 90% of first-year students live in one of our 39 residence halls, while College Park counts a huge variety of appealing off-campus options for Terps at any stage of their academic journey. Students also appreciate the convenience and tastiness of the healthy food at UMD. Our award-winning dining program is recognized for its nutrition, sustainability focus and facility design.

Athletics

Maryland offers more than 300 degree-granting programs, many of them ranked among the nation’s best. Terps embrace opportunities beyond campus in D.C., across Maryland and around the globe to expand their learning and perspectives.

Clubs and Organizations

UMD has more than 800 clubs and groups, so students can find the one—or 10!—that’s right for them. It’s a great way to get involved on campus, dive into a new interest, share yours and make friends.

Honoring Our State

As Maryland’s flagship institution, UMD plays a critical role in every part of the state’s economy. Each year, we graduate thousands of students who strengthen the workforce. An engine for innovation, we attract investment, launch successful startups and stimulate job creation. We also share our expertise and collaborate with communities on issues such as health care, education and the environment, all to make Maryland thrive.
